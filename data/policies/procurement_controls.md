# Procurement Controls Policy

## PO Reconciliation Requirement
All invoices must be reconciled against an approved purchase order before payment release. The approved vendor, currency, PO identifier, validity date window, and remaining PO balance must match the invoice.

## Amount Tolerance
Invoices may be auto-approved only when the invoice total is within the configured tolerance of the PO approved total and does not exceed the remaining PO balance.

## High-Value Approval
Invoices above INR 25,000 require finance controller approval even when automated controls pass.

## Audit Evidence
The workflow must retain the extracted invoice fields, reconciliation findings, policy context, approval route, and fraud signals as audit evidence.
