# Fraud and Duplicate Invoice Policy

## Duplicate Invoice Controls
Potential duplicate invoices must be flagged when invoice ID, vendor ID, PO ID, invoice amount, normalized document fingerprint, or invoice date closely resembles a prior payment request.

## Vendor Risk
Vendors on a watchlist or vendors with high baseline risk require additional approval and evidence documentation.

## Rolling Window Monitoring
Finance operations should monitor rolling 30/60/90 day volume and value concentration. Sudden spikes in invoice volume or total value can indicate split billing, duplicate submission, or procurement leakage.

## False Positive Management
Fraud alerts should be ranked by severity and accompanied by explanation so reviewers can quickly dismiss benign cases and focus on high-risk invoices.
